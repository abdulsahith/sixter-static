Get Started in Seconds

After downloading and installing Sixter, open your terminal or command prompt and run:

Terminal 
sixter tunnel --subdomain <your subdomain name> --port <your project port>

Example

sixter tunnel --subdomain myapp --port 3000

This will create a secure tunnel at myapp.sixter.in pointing to your local port 3000
